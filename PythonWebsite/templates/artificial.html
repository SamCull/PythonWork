Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.
Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision.
There are 4 types of Artifical Intelligence:
  • Reactive
  • Limited Memory
  • Theory of Mind
  • Self-Aware
Good and bad advantages of Artificial Intelligence:

Good advantages:
  1. Reduction in Human Error
  2. Takes risks instead of humans
  3. Can work 24/7

Bad advantages:
  1. More costly
  2. Unemployment
  3. Lacks Out of Box Thinking
